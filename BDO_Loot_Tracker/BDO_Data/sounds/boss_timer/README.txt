Boss Timer Sound Files
======================

Place your custom sound files in this folder to use with the Boss Timer sound notifications.

Required sound files (can be .mp3 or .wav format):
- 30min.mp3 - Plays 30 minutes before boss spawn
- 15min.mp3 - Plays 15 minutes before boss spawn
- 10min.mp3 - Plays 10 minutes before boss spawn
- 5min.mp3 - Plays 5 minutes before boss spawn
- 3min.mp3 - Plays 3 minutes before boss spawn
- 1min.mp3 - Plays 1 minute before boss spawn
- spawn.mp3 - Plays when boss spawns

Notes:
- The app will look for .mp3 files first, then .wav files if .mp3 is not found
- Make sure to enable the sound notification checkboxes in the Boss Timer tab
- Only sounds for enabled notifications will play
